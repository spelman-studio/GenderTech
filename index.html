ABOUT GENDER

What is it?

Sex

All humans are born with biological characteristics of sex, either male, female, or intersex.

Gender

Gender, however, is a social construct and generally based on the norms, behaviors, and societal roles expected of individuals based primarily on their sex.

Identity

Gender identity describes a person’s self-perceived gender, which could be male, female, or otherwise.

Continuum

Gender continuum, in the study of human sexuality, the thesis that gender is not “binary,” or limited to the specific genders “man” and “woman” (or “boy” and “girl”), but continuous, forming a spectrum.
